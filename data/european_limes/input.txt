Europe has crossed the Rubicon. Artificial intelligence is becoming infrastructure.
Europe must be able to study it, host it, improve it, govern it, and trust it.

Limes Labs is early. We are not claiming capability yet. We are organizing capability.

We believe Europe needs open intelligence infrastructure: open source, open weights,
transparent safety, European roots, global collaboration.

Public knowledge should create public value. Open source must win. Open weights must win.

The Roman limes was not only a border. It was a system of roads, signals, trade, and coordination.
Today the frontier is artificial intelligence. Europe needs the infrastructure to stand on its own.

EuroHPC, AI Factories, and IT4LIA already exist. The gap is coordination.
We map compute, contact programs, gather contributors, build tools, test models, publish results.

We are not claiming a frontier model on day one. We start with maps, benchmarks, deployment guides,
model cards, safety templates, and small reproducible experiments.

Researchers: clear problems, public credit, reproducible work, a path to European impact.
Investors and institutions: AI is strategic infrastructure. Europe is underbuilt.
